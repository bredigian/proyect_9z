WE ARE 9Z TEAM

An organization dedicated to the most recognized eSports worldwide. We recently accomplished a major milestone that any team wants to have in their showcase, and that was qualifying for the Antwerp CS:GO Major 2022.

We are a team from Argentina. 9z was created on August 18, 2018, thanks to Francisco Postiglione, or as we know him, Frankkaster.

Currently we have a CS:GO team, CS:GO Academy, VALORANT and League Of Legends.
